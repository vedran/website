Pricefox

Pricefox was a mobile app that allowed anyone to find products and compare prices at nearby retail locations.

Users could search for an individual product by scanning the UPC, or create a whole shopping list to compare the total price of an entire shopping trip at multiple retailers at the same time.

We built the product and price databases by collecting, deduplicating, and combining data from several sources including crowd sourcing, manual scanning, and crawling of online retail flyers.

I developed the Android app and the complete backend system that powered the project, including the system design, search architecture, and web crawling.

Cheeky

Cheeky was a mobile app that combined Instagram with fashion product placement in a way that allowed users to get rewards for their selfies.

Users could post a photo, tag the fashion and makeup products they were wearing, and then earn rewards when other users purchased products through their referrals. Rewards were offered in the form of gift cards or paypal transfers.

Our product database was built up through affiliate partnerships with hundreds of retailers including Forever 21, Banana Republic, Walmart, and Target. This amounted tens of millions of unique products that were stored and taggable.

I developed both Android and iOS apps, the website, and the backend system that powered the entire project.
